More room in History, and a reset for the counters.

## What's new

- History now keeps your last 500 transcripts (up from 50), shows 20 at a time with Show More, and can export the whole lot to a text file.
- Reset Stats: start the dictation counters from zero whenever you like.

## Install

1. Download Goji.dmg below.
2. Open it and drag Goji to the Applications folder.
3. Launch Goji. Grant Microphone (prompt) and Accessibility (System Settings > Privacy & Security > Accessibility).

Requires macOS 14 or later. Apple Silicon recommended.
