Your words, at scale.

Goji 1.1 is about one thing: getting your replacement rules built in seconds instead of one painful entry at a time.

## What's new

- Suggest Variations: type a word Goji mishears ("Jira"), click Suggest, and approve the likely mishearings as rules in one go. You pick every rule; nothing is added silently. Needs Apple Intelligence.
- Word packs: export your rules and Names & phrases as one JSON file, import someone else's (merges, never deletes). Packs can be written by hand, by scripts, or by AI agents. Grab the [Tech Starter Pack](https://brendanrong.github.io/goji/tech-starter-pack.json) for instant fixes like "git hub" → GitHub, "a sink" → async, and "Cooper Netties" → Kubernetes.
- New rule from History: hit + on any transcript, click the word that came out wrong, type the fix. It becomes an ordinary replacement rule you can see and delete.
- Stats in History: dictated words, time saved, day streak, and average speed. All counted locally, like everything else.

## Install

1. Download Goji.dmg below.
2. Open it and drag Goji to the Applications folder.
3. Launch Goji. Grant Microphone (prompt) and Accessibility (System Settings > Privacy & Security > Accessibility).

Requires macOS 14 or later. Apple Silicon recommended.
