Pick your model, duck your music, choose your sounds.

## What's new

- Models pane: choose which speech model transcribes for you. Parakeet v3 stays the default; Parakeet v2 (English-only) and Cohere Transcribe (larger, strongest accuracy, English for now) are one click to download, switch, or remove. Every model runs fully on your Mac.
- Storage transparency: see exactly how much disk the models use and open the folder in Finder.
- "While dictating" now has three modes: Do Nothing, Quieter (ducks your speakers to 20% and restores them, Siri style), or Pause Media. Quieter falls back to Pause on outputs without volume control.
- Sound packs: Minimal (soft blips), Wood (marimba taps), or Classic (the macOS pops).

## Install

1. Download Goji.dmg below.
2. Open it and drag Goji to the Applications folder.
3. Launch Goji. Grant Microphone (prompt) and Accessibility (System Settings > Privacy & Security > Accessibility).

Requires macOS 14 or later. Apple Silicon recommended.
