Record your own shortcut.

## What's new

- Custom shortcut combos: pick "Custom Combo…" in Settings > General, click Record Keys, hold any mix of Fn, Control, Option, Command, and Shift (left and right count as different keys), and release. Hold Fn + Right Control to dictate if that's your thing.
- All hints and the menu bar now show your actual shortcut, whatever it is.

## Install

1. Download Goji.dmg below.
2. Open it and drag Goji to the Applications folder.
3. Launch Goji. Grant Microphone (prompt) and Accessibility (System Settings > Privacy & Security > Accessibility).

Requires macOS 14 or later. Apple Silicon recommended.
