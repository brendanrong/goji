The HUD anchors to where you're actually typing.

## What's fixed

- The recording indicator could still land on the wrong screen (bottom edge of a stacked monitor) after a restart or display change. It positioned against the "main" screen, which for a background app means the primary display, not the one you're typing on. It now asks Accessibility where the focused text field is and anchors there, falling back to the mouse's screen.

## Install

1. Download Goji.dmg below.
2. Open it and drag Goji to the Applications folder.
3. Launch Goji. Grant Microphone (prompt) and Accessibility (System Settings > Privacy & Security > Accessibility).

Requires macOS 14 or later. Apple Silicon recommended.
