Your words, recognized at the source.

## What's new

- Enhanced recognition: Names & phrases now corrects your words inside the speech model itself. A small helper model listens for your terms in the audio and fixes mishearings using acoustic evidence, not text guesswork. Say Jachin, get Jachin, even with AI cleanup off. One-time ~150 MB download in Settings > Transcription; works with the Parakeet models.
- The Transcription pane now reads in pipeline order (your words, cleanup, formatting, replacements), and each section says whether it needs Apple Intelligence. Only AI cleanup does.

## Install

1. Download Goji.dmg below.
2. Open it and drag Goji to the Applications folder.
3. Launch Goji. Grant Microphone (prompt) and Accessibility (System Settings > Privacy & Security > Accessibility).

Requires macOS 14 or later. Apple Silicon recommended.
