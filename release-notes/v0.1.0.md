Goji's first release. Local, private dictation for macOS: hold a key, talk, release, and your words paste where your cursor is. Everything runs on your Mac.

## What's in it

- Hold Right Option, speak, release. The transcript pastes into whatever app you're in.
- Fully on-device transcription with Parakeet v3 on the Apple Neural Engine. Nothing leaves your Mac.
- Menu bar app, no Dock clutter, with an optional Dock icon.
- Hold or Toggle mode, a configurable shortcut, and Esc to cancel.
- Notch or panel recording HUD with a live waveform.
- Word replacements, local transcript history (last 50), and start and stop sound cues.
- Optional on-device AI cleanup on macOS 26 with Apple Intelligence, off by default.
- Microphone picker with a live test meter.

## Reliability

- Rebuilds the audio engine per recording, so it survives sleep, wake, and mic device changes without a restart.
- Preserves your existing clipboard (text, images, files) across the paste.
- Robust hotkey handling so a held modifier can't strand a recording.

## Install

1. Download Goji.dmg below.
2. Open it and drag Goji to your Applications folder.
3. Launch Goji. Grant Microphone (prompt) and Accessibility (System Settings > Privacy & Security > Accessibility).
4. On first launch it downloads the model once (about 600 MB). After that it works offline.

Requires macOS 14 or later. Apple Silicon recommended.
