Tidier About, and a hint for stuck permissions.

## What's new

- About, tightened: everything lines up left like the rest of Settings, no more floating in space.
- Gentler first run: the welcome window can no longer open buried behind other windows, and permissions arrive one at a time. Microphone at launch, Accessibility only once the model is downloaded and Goji is ready to paste.
- Recording display picker: choose Panel or Notch in Settings > General with visual previews. This option existed under the hood but had no UI, so everyone was stuck on the bottom panel. Notch is now the default: it hugs the camera housing on MacBooks and draws its own island on external displays.
- If pasting ever stops while the Accessibility toggle looks on, the menu now tells you the fix: remove Goji from the list and add it back.

## Install

1. Download Goji.dmg below.
2. Open it and drag Goji to the Applications folder.
3. Launch Goji. Grant Microphone (prompt) and Accessibility (System Settings > Privacy & Security > Accessibility).

Requires macOS 14 or later. Apple Silicon recommended.
