A friendlier first run, and the notch by default.

## What's new

- Recording display picker: choose Panel or Notch in Settings > General. This option existed under the hood but had no UI, so everyone was stuck on the bottom panel. Notch is now the default: it hugs the camera housing on MacBooks and draws its own island on external displays.
- Gentler first run: the welcome window can no longer open buried behind other windows, and permissions arrive one at a time. Microphone at launch, Accessibility only once the model is downloaded and Goji is ready to paste.

## Install

1. Download Goji.dmg below.
2. Open it and drag Goji to the Applications folder.
3. Launch Goji. Grant Microphone (prompt) and Accessibility (System Settings > Privacy & Security > Accessibility).

Requires macOS 14 or later. Apple Silicon recommended.
