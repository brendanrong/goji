Dictate over music, pick from more shortcuts, and an AI cleanup that behaves.

## What's new

- Mute other audio while dictating: an optional toggle that silences your speakers during recording so music doesn't bleed into the mic, and restores them right after. Works on outputs without a mute control (external monitors) by dropping the volume instead.
- More recording shortcuts: Fn (Globe) and the Shift keys join Option, Command, and Control.
- AI cleanup now edits your words instead of replying to them or rewriting whole sentences, and a sanity check ships the raw transcript whenever the model goes off script.
- Cleaner settings: every option now has a short description under its label, and About gains Private by design, Report a Problem, and Buy Me a Coffee.

## Install

1. Download Goji.dmg below.
2. Open it and drag Goji to the Applications folder.
3. Launch Goji. Grant Microphone (prompt) and Accessibility (System Settings > Privacy & Security > Accessibility).

Requires macOS 14 or later. Apple Silicon recommended.
