Goji tells you when there's a new Goji.

## What's new

- Update checker: Goji asks GitHub once a day whether a newer version exists (toggle in Settings > About). When one does, the menu bar and About show it, and one click downloads the new DMG. Nothing about you or your dictations is sent.
- Pause media while dictating: replaces "mute other audio". Pauses whatever's playing (Music, Spotify, browser video) and resumes it after, which works on every output device, including monitors with no volume control.
- New sound cues: soft minimal blips, rising for start and falling for stop, replacing the system sounds that read as glitchy on quick dictations.
- Formatting option: remove the full stop the model adds at the end of every dictation (Settings > Transcription).
- The installer window now walks you through it: Goji on the left, an arrow, Applications on the right, and a drag hint underneath.

## Install

1. Download Goji.dmg below.
2. Open it and drag Goji to the Applications folder.
3. Launch Goji. Grant Microphone (prompt) and Accessibility (System Settings > Privacy & Security > Accessibility).

Requires macOS 14 or later. Apple Silicon recommended.
