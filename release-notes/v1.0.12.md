## What's new

- About, redesigned: app icon front and center, version and tagline, "Signed and notarized by Apple" and privacy badges, and the update and feedback buttons in one tidy row.

If you're on Goji 1.0.11, this is the first update that installs itself: one click in the menu bar and Goji swaps itself out and relaunches.

## Install

1. Download Goji.dmg below.
2. Open it and drag Goji to the Applications folder.
3. Launch Goji. Grant Microphone (prompt) and Accessibility (System Settings > Privacy & Security > Accessibility).

Requires macOS 14 or later. Apple Silicon recommended.
