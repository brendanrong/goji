Teach Goji your words.

## What's new

- Names & phrases: add the words Goji keeps mishearing once (people, teams, product names, jargon) in Settings > Transcription, and AI cleanup nudges close mishearings to the exact spelling. "Jaken" becomes "Jachin" without a pile of replacement rules. Needs AI cleanup on.
- Simpler settings: the important choice (your shortcut) sits alone at the top of General, tuning options moved under Fine-tuning, and Microphone now lives inside General. Five panes instead of six.
- The update check now says when it couldn't reach GitHub instead of pretending you're up to date, and re-checks every time you open About.

## Install

1. Download Goji.dmg below.
2. Open it and drag Goji to the Applications folder.
3. Launch Goji. Grant Microphone (prompt) and Accessibility (System Settings > Privacy & Security > Accessibility).

Requires macOS 14 or later. Apple Silicon recommended.
