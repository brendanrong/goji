The notch HUD follows you between monitors.

## What's fixed

- Moving to another display no longer strands the recording indicator at stale coordinates (which could look like it fell to the bottom of the screen on stacked monitor arrangements). The HUD now re-anchors to the screen you're dictating into at the start of every recording.

## Install

1. Download Goji.dmg below.
2. Open it and drag Goji to the Applications folder.
3. Launch Goji. Grant Microphone (prompt) and Accessibility (System Settings > Privacy & Security > Accessibility).

Requires macOS 14 or later. Apple Silicon recommended.
