Goji

Local, private dictation for macOS. Hold a key, talk, release. Your words appear where your cursor is.

Download for macOS View on GitHub

Requires macOS 14 or later. Apple Silicon recommended.

Listening…

Why Goji

Nothing leaves your Mac

Speech is transcribed on-device with Parakeet v3. No cloud, no accounts, no telemetry.

Fast enough to feel instant

Runs on the Apple Neural Engine. A short utterance lands as text about a second after you let go.

Works everywhere you type

Pastes into Slack, Chrome, Notes, Xcode, anything. Your clipboard is put back afterward.

Yours to shape

Hold or toggle, pick your shortcut, notch or panel HUD, word replacements, and local history.

Release history

v1.0.7July 6, 2026

Make Goji yours: light, dark, or system.

  • Appearance setting: system, light, or dark, applied instantly.
  • Much faster first-run model download, with real progress instead of long stalls.
  • Pause media while dictating: pauses your music or video and resumes it after, on any output device.
  • Settings controls sized like System Settings, and a fixed light mode sidebar.
v1.0.6July 6, 2026

Record your own shortcut.

  • Custom shortcut combos: hold any mix of Fn, Control, Option, Command, and Shift, and that's your dictation key now.
v1.0.5July 6, 2026

Dictate over music, more shortcuts, and an AI cleanup that behaves.

  • Optionally mute other audio while dictating, so music doesn't bleed into the mic. Works on external monitors too.
  • More recording shortcuts: Fn (Globe) and the Shift keys.
  • AI cleanup now edits your words instead of rewriting them.
  • Cleaner settings: every option explains itself, plus feedback and support links in About.
v1.0.4July 6, 2026

The notch HUD now looks the same on every display.

  • External monitors get a synthetic notch island, same design as on the MacBook, instead of a floating pill.
  • The DMG now includes an Applications shortcut for standard drag-and-drop installs.
v1.0.3July 5, 2026

Hands-free dictation and faster mic switching.

  • Double-tap the hotkey to lock recording hands-free. Tap again to finish.
  • Microphone picker in the menu bar: see and switch your mic without opening Settings.
  • The AI cleanup setting now explains exactly why it's unavailable, instead of a generic message.
v1.0.2July 5, 2026

A menu bar icon that's actually Goji, and builds anyone can install.

  • The menu bar icon is now the goji berry mark: outline while idle, filled while recording.
  • Builds are signed and notarized, so downloads open first try with no Gatekeeper warnings.
v1.0.1July 5, 2026

A friendlier first run, and the Dock icon now does something.

  • First launch asks before the one-time model download and shows live progress.
  • Clicking the Dock icon opens Settings instead of doing nothing.
  • Download state and retry live in the menu bar.
v1.0.0July 5, 2026

Goji hits 1.0. A redesigned settings window, a seamless notch HUD, and fixes for every way the app could freeze or fight the app you're typing in.

  • Settings, rebuilt: sidebar navigation in a proper window that opens reliably on macOS 26.
  • Seamless notch HUD: exactly notch height, tight wings, and it shows the app you're dictating into.
  • Fixed a freeze that could lock the app up after opening Settings or mid-dictation.
  • Esc cancels a recording without leaking into the app you're dictating into.
  • More reliable pasting into Electron apps like Slack and Claude.
  • Sound cues no longer drop when dictations come in fast.
v0.1.0July 5, 2026

First release. The core loop, a lean settings window, and enough polish to use every day.

  • Hold Right Option, speak, release. Transcript pastes into the frontmost app.
  • Fully on-device transcription with Parakeet v3. Nothing leaves your Mac.
  • Menu bar app with an optional Dock icon. Hold or Toggle mode, Esc cancels.
  • Notch or panel recording HUD with a live waveform.
  • Word replacements, local transcript history, and start and stop sound cues.
  • Optional on-device AI cleanup on macOS 26 with Apple Intelligence.
  • Microphone picker with a live test meter.
  • Reliability: survives sleep, wake, and mic changes, and preserves your existing clipboard.
